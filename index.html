What is Zolucion?

Zolucion Inno Asia Limited is a fully licensed insurance broker company incorporated in Hong Kong with line of business in long term (including linked long term), medical/health insurance and general insurance. We aim to provide a digital platform to our customers access to right personal related insurance services in Hong Kong as the point of entry into an ecosystem that links high quality professional financial advisors to source and recommend suitable insurance products.
